My research and projects

Vikrant More, Sarthak Kaingade, Pradeep Gaidhani, Nitin Gupta, "Visual odometry using optic flow for unmanned aerial vehicles", IEEE Proceedings of 2015 International Conference on Cognitive Computing and Information Processing. Available here. I worked on this project to demonstrate fully autonomous behaviour of a quadrotor using a vision sensor for position estimation. You can check out it's demo.

Also worked on another cool project where the quadcopter could identify a colored object and follow it. Sorry I don't have a video of this project, but if you believe me - it worked! Wrote a small paper on this which was accepted at an IEEE conference, but I didn't have the resources to make it there.

Tools I love: ROS, Gazebo, OpenCV.
